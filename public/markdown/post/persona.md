# Twitter Persona
## Name
Tom Teddy
## Technology
### Devices
* Iphone 16 Pro using the latest operating system and Safari
* Macbook Pro 13 inch  Sequoia 15.4 Safari
### Love/Hate
* A lover or technology who loves connecting with new people who share their interests 
* Has a smart house that ties directly to their Iphone so they can turn on the lights before they get home. Access security cameras so they can see who is outside their house without going to the door and control the temperature to save on their heating and cooling bills
### Proficiency
* Moderate user who can handle most user flows but can sometimes get overwhelmed with more complex tasks
* Prefers using their phone to a computer since they work an office job and being in front of computer reminds them of being at work
## Attitudes and Behaviors
* Follow tech gurus to stay up to date with new Smart house technology that may be coming out
* Wants to stay up to date with popular trends since everything seems to start on Twitter
* Has a fear of missing out and wants to stay up to date with the latest news ad trends
* Twitter is easier to navigate
* Isn't interested in seeing influencers sharing photos of what they are doing
* Doesn't want to be bombarded with updates from friends and family.
* Prefers text based updates to video.

## User Story
As a power user, I want to access my favorite content creator's for a review on the new Ring Door Bell

## Use-case
Tom heard from a friend at work that Mishaal Rahman has posted a review of the new ring doorbell on twitter and wants to go go read the review and other users opinions

### Precondition: 
* Tom is logged in to his Twitter account and is on the home page
### Post condition: 
* Tom leaves twitter and watches the long form video from Mishaal

## Interaction Flow
* Tom scans the home page and clicks into the search field at the top of the page
* Twitter opens a results box asking tom to search fo people lists or keywords
* Tom types Mi
* Twitter displays trending results and a list of users in the search box
* Tom finishes typing mishal containing a typo 
* Twitter is able to use fuzzy search to list only people with Mishaal as the top result
* Tom clicks Mishaal
* Twitter redirects Tom to Mishaals profile page
* Tom scrolls down Mishaals posts to find the post he is looking for and stops on the correct post
* Twitter adds a slight background to encourage Tom to click on the post
* Tom clicks on the post
* Twitter takes Tom to the detailed page for that tweet
* Tom scrolls and reads comments on the post
* Twitter loads more posts as Tom Scrolls
* Tom scroll up to click on a link for more details
* Twitter saves the link Tom clicks into its database and redirects tom to the new website.
